Sea Side Park Hotel

Sea Side Park Hotel sits on a rocky outcrop on the southern coast of Sri Lanka near the old fort town of Matara , a sentinel of luxury in a stunning landscape of calming sunrises and psychedelic sunsets, caressed by the breezes that waft across the still waters of the river, serenaded by birdcall and the swishing of oars, bathed in a glow that only paradise can offer you.Golden-hued beach fringed by coconut palms overlooks the roaring Indian Ocean. This is ‘Sea Side Beach Park.’

With spacious, well-appointed luxury rooms, unparalleled international and local cuisine and a personalised service offered to individual guests, Sea side beach park has a wide ranging portfolio of experiences, products and services to offer its discerning guests.There’s never a dull moment at Sea Side Park Hotel ! Welcome to Ses Side Park Hotel, a small luxury hotel on the South Coast of Sri Lanka!
